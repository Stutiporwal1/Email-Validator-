About Us

Welcome to iValidateE - your reliable, real-time email validation solution. Our goal is to make email verification simple, fast, and secure for individuals, developers, and businesses.

iValidateE is built to help solve a common problem (that is not so common in the world) : users entering fake or invalid email addresses.

Our mission is to offer an easy-to-use tool that fits into any workflow. We are always listening and working on the feedbacks to improve the iValidateE even more better.

Thank you for using iValidateE. We're glad you're here!
