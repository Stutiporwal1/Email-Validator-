About Us

Welcome to iValidateE - your reliable, real-time email validation solution. Our goal is to make email verification simple, fast, and secure for individuals, developers, and businesses.

iValidate is built to help solve a common problem (that is not so common in the world) : users entering fake or invalid email addresses.

What makes us different? We focus on privacy-first validation. We don’t store or share any emails you check — everything runs in real time, ensuring fast results and total confidentiality.

Our mission is to offer a lightweight, easy-to-use tool that fits into any workflow — from simple contact forms to professional SaaS platforms. We're always improving and listening to feedback to make iValidateE even better.
